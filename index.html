Shortly About Us

Do you remember when learning music was all about having a boring teacher making you do boring exercises for months and months until you get frustrated? at PY ROCK those times are all gone, we offer a modern music education where students actually have fun as they learn music. Students love our classes so much that teachers are not only teachers anymore, they are good friends and mentors that the students can count on. We constantly upgrade our teaching method using the latest technology available with the most powerfull tools; online videos resources, computer softwares, phone applications, highly trained teachers, we got them all!.
