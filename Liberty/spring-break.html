Day 1

Explore

Students get to know each other with team building activities and games , share experiences, explore different instrument then decide the main instrument they will be playing at the show.

Day 2-3

Ensemble

The teacher breaks the song down into pieces and start working on musical ensemble with the band, each student get different part and the hard work begins.

Day 4

Record

After 15 hours of hard work the song is ready to be record, a talented producer visits the school to record the bands songs!. They also learn how to master the performance anxiety and stage fright by applying different techniques.

Day 5

Show Time

On the last day, the campers have their on private show with professional equipment, lights, hosts, 3+ guest bands and a lot of fun!
