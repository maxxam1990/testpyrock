Guitar Lessons

Learning to play guitar requires discipline, consistency and well-structured guitar lessons plan. PY rock music lessons include 30-50 minutes session, in either a private or group setting. Daily practice is essential to retain muscle memory and our instructor know that, for that reason they use real rock songs for the guitar lessons in order to motivate students to do their practice at home. Addicionally, once they reach certain level the get to be in a rock band where the real learning and fun begins!.
